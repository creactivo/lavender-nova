Let's talk about it

We meet a man some features of this strange designer.

where does he live?

Recidente in Cartagena-Colombia. Graphic Designer, Frontend Developer and Illustrator.

where did he study?

Graduated in 2007 from the School of Fine Arts in Cartagena de Indias. with an experience of 9 years in different areas of design as: Project Director, creative, print designer, illustrator and even frontend web developer and designer and I think that makes clear the versatility of speaking in the third person.

what are your tastes?

He has always liked everything related to the image, so I started to design and illustrate a very early age, feel much satisfaction by small but no less important details when performing any kind of work.


He likes develop collaborative approaches with customers, created the enabling environment for foster a productive partnership.

conclusion

Resulting in intelligent, efficient, clear experiences, beautiful and useful.

services and skills
